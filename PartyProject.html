Custom Dice

The dice I have worked on is pretty straight forward right now. I just used a cube and added some sphere's to each side to correct the numbers. I added some custom box colliders to each side of the dice to register what side of the dice it landed on. I also added a projectile movement component to add velocity to the dice that instantiates from the camera. To add some rotation to the dice when it is rolled I added a Physics Thruster to the dice in the corner to have it spin in a random rotation when rolled.
